Future Projects (Hopefully, Under Construction)

Homemade Street Food

I would love to make a webapp that allows users to order homemade street food by various home cooks to bring various street food culture to everybody.

  • Home cooks will be able to advertise their menu as if they are restaurants on the webapp
  • Users can choose between various street food available to them just like how they would order takeaway / delivery food online
  • Biggest challenge would be quality control, and hygiene control

Deals Alert

Another project would be one that alerts myself or other users of potential bargains of products within our wishlist as soon as it goes below a certain price point.

  • Might need to test with biggest e-commerce sites first
  • A good starting point would be something similar to OzBargain but personalised to user
  • Biggest challenge would be how to account for various retailers / sellers for the same product
